EditoriaLies

An extension for Google Chrome that allows you to edit the text of a webpage. Because sometimes you know better.
  • Pin the extension.
  • Select text on the active tab to replace.
  • Click on the extension icon in the toolbar to open the popup menu.
  • Selected text will appear in the popup window's "Find this text:" field.
  • If no text was selected, enter the text you want to replace in the "Find this text:" field.
  • Then enter the replacement text in the "Replace text abve with" field.
  • Click on the "Editorialize" button.
  • The active tab will reload and revise the webpage as indicated.
  • This should work on most news websites, and probably won't on social media sites that obfuscate their structure.
